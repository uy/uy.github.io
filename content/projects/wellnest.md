## Your Personal Health Companion

This app turns your Apple Health data into clear, friendly, and meaningful insights — all processed **locally on your device**.

Instead of overwhelming charts or clinical language, you receive calm, supportive summaries that help you better understand your daily habits, short-term patterns, and long-term trends.

### What You’ll Get
- Daily, weekly, and monthly health insights
- Easy-to-understand summaries of activity, sleep, and wellness signals
- Positive reinforcement to keep you motivated
- Gentle suggestions to support healthier routines

### Designed With Care
- **Privacy-first:** Your health data stays on your device
- **No diagnoses or treatments:** This app does not replace medical professionals
- **Supportive tone:** No judgment, no pressure — just awareness

### Built for Everyday Wellness
Whether you want a quick daily reflection or a broader view of your habits over time, this app helps you stay mindful of your well-being in a simple and reassuring way.

Your data. Your device. Your health — explained with clarity and care.
